Shoulders and abs workout

Here you will find shoulders and abs workout.

5 recommended exercises for shoulders:

  1. Shoulder Press: Stand or sit with a dumbbell or barbell held at shoulder level, palms facing forward. Press the weight upward, extending your arms fully without locking your elbows. Lower the weight back down to the starting position.


  2. Dumbbell Lateral Raises: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, hold a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing your body. Raise your arms out to the sides until they are parallel to the ground, keeping a slight bend in your elbows. Lower the weights back down in a controlled manner.


  3. Bent-Over Dumbbell Reverse Flyes: Bend your knees slightly and hinge forward at the hips while keeping your back straight. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with palms facing each other. Lift your arms out to the sides and squeeze your shoulder blades together. Lower the weights back down and repeat.


  4. Arnold Press: Sit or stand with dumbbells held at shoulder level, palms facing your body. Rotate your palms outward as you press the weights overhead. At the top of the movement, your palms should be facing forward. Reverse the motion to return to the starting position.


  5. Upright Rows: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, hold a barbell or dumbbells in front of your thighs, palms facing your body. Lift the weights straight up, leading with your elbows, until they reach shoulder height. Lower the weights back down in a controlled manner.


5 recommended exercises for abs:

  1. Reverse Crunches: Lie on your back with your hands by your sides and your knees bent, feet lifted off the ground. Engage your core and curl your knees towards your chest, lifting your hips off the ground. Slowly lower your hips back down to the starting position.


  2. Bicycle Crunches: Lie on your back with your hands behind your head and your knees bent. Lift your shoulders off the ground and bring your right elbow towards your left knee while extending your right leg straight. Twist to bring your left elbow towards your right knee while extending your left leg straight. Continue alternating sides in a cycling motion.

  3. Plank with Knee Tucks: Begin in a high plank position with your hands directly under your shoulders and your body in a straight line. Lift one foot off the ground and bring your knee towards your chest, engaging your core. Return your foot back to the starting position and repeat with the other leg.


  4. Hanging Leg Raises: Hang from a pull-up bar with your arms fully extended. Keeping your legs straight, engage your core and lift your legs up until they are parallel to the ground. Hold for a moment, then lower them back down with control.


  5. Dragon Flag: Lie on a bench, hold onto the edge behind your head, and lift your legs and torso off the bench. Straighten your body, keeping it in a straight line from head to toes, and slowly lower your body down until your feet are just above the bench. Pause, then engage your core to lift your body back up to the starting position.
